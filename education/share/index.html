We are creating a calendar of activities for sharing skills and creativity, for everyone in the community. We invite you to fill in the form below if you would like to share an event. Information you submit won't be publicly displayed; it will be used by facilitators to plan the calendar.

Types of activities

  • Workshops: Something you'd like to share with a group of people, whether it's knowledge, practical skills or a topic you wish to explore with others.
  • Events: Community events, such as fundraisers, seminars, themed parties, carnivals, presentations, music evenings, sharing of sports or other physical pursuits, like walking or climbing.
  • Work Alongside: Where you invite one or more people to work with you, and maybe to assist with something you enjoy. It is a hands-on way for others to learn skills and share in your knowledge, to experiment with and explore different ways of working, and to be creative in an authentic environment.
